What’s the Data Observability Conf about?

The DataObservabilityConf is India's first meeting of experts, where future trends and career opportunities in DataOps are discussed.

Leading experts in Data and AI will present the full scope of DataOps Observability and be ready to answer your hardest questions. Learn new techniques, share your skills, and interact with renowned developers in the data domain.

DataOps professionals will deepen their knowledge of Data Engineering, the latest DataOps trends, and Observability for modern data stacks.

faq

FAQ

  • 1 Is Azure Kubernetes Day free of cost?

    Yes, the conference is absolutely FREE to attend.

  • 2 How do I attend the Azure Kubernetes Day conference?

    Azure Kubernetes Day is an online conference that you can attend from anywhere. The whole conference will be streamed to YouTube and other social media platforms. The live session link will be shared with registered participants a day before the event.

  • 3 Can I ask questions in between the sessions?

    Yes, you can ask questions to our speakers by commenting on the chat stream. The speaker will answer those questions after their session ends.

  • 4 How do I get notified for the conference and get links to join the conference?

    Once you register for the conference we will keep you notified with all the details regarding the conference including the links to join the conference.

  • 5 I run a community. How to make it a community partner? What is the benefit?

    The objective of most of the developer communities is to help developers learn, upskill or adopt latest and best - and that’s the objective of the conference as well! We invite community partners to support each other in spreading the word about the conference in your community and in return, the conference will provide wider visibility to your community!
    From past experience with similar conferences, Azure Kubernetes Day Conference website will have tens of thousands of visitors. Your community will get more visibility by becoming a community partner. In return, we expect you to share details about the conference in your meetup group, email lists and in social media. Just drop us a mail here if you are interested in making your’s a community partner for the Azure Kubernetes Day.

  • 6 I am a speaker and interested in speaking in the conference. How do I submit the CFP?

    Thank you for your interest in speaking in the conference. Please submit your CFP here. If your session is selected by our review panel, we’ll get in touch with you to schedule your session in the conference.

  • 7 I am a student - will I get a participation certificate when I attend this conference?

    Yes, we do provide a participation certificate to those who are interested. You need to attend the conference to receive it. Towards the end of the conference, we’ll share a feedback link - in that you can mention you need the participation certificate and we’ll send it to you within 3 days after the conference.

  • 8 Can I get the supporting material (slides, code, etc) and the video recordings after the event?

    Yes, of course. We’ll share the learning material in a GitHub link. The session video recordings and links to slides will be shared in this same website after the conference.
